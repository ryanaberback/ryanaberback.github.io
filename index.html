About Me

I am the research coordinator for the Stress and Developmental Psychopathology Lab at Concordia University. Currently, I am investigating how the hormone oxytocin influences interpersonal behaviour in individuals with major depressive disorder.

I am a Co-Founder of the Concordia Journal of Psychology and Neuroscience, and I currently serve as the Journal’s Co-Editor in Chief.

Favourite Quote: “He is happy whose circumstances suit his temper, but he is more excellent who can suit his temper to his circumstance.” - David Hume
